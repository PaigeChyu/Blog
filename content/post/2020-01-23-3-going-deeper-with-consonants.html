Instructor: This keyword helps you to remember the letter p. Every time you see this letter, I want you to see the picture of a pup in your mind, and I want you to hear the sound of the first letter. This picture will unlock the sound of this letter just as a key unlocks the door.

Instructor: Say its sound, not its name.

Continue the script method with r.

Use the following keywords to help your child learn the p and r

Pp: pup
Rr: rat

Blending table for P and R

r p r p r p
ra pa ra pa ra Pa
ra t pa d ra g pa t ra p Pa m
rat pad rag pat rap Pam

After your child maters the words in the above table, have him read the following sentences for practice.

Pam had a map.

Dad had a fat ram.

A rat had a pad.
